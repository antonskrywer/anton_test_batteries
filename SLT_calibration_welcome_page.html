Welcome to this Music Study!

This music psychology study investigates your musical abilities.

Your Task is to complete a music test were you have to listen to and evaluate melodies.
The study also includes a questionnaire about your musical knowledge and your demographic background.

The study takes roughly 15 minutes to complete. Please make sure that you are in a quiet environment and use headphones or high-quality speakers.

If you have any questions about the study, please contact anton.schreiber@uni-hamburg
